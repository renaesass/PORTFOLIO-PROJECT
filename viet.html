Book Halong Bay Cruise

Halong Bay is a beautiful natural wonder in northern Vietnam near the Chinese border. The Bay is dotted with 1,600 limestone islands and islets and covers an area of over 1,500 sqkm. This extraordinary area was declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1994.


Recepies To Try at Home

Vietnamese street food is one of the highlights of travelling in Southeast Asia! From Hanoi to Ho Chi Minh City, the streets are alive with the smells and sizzling sounds of locals cooking delicious Vietnamese dishes right by the roadside!


Find Hotels in Hoi An

Graceful, historic Hoi An is Vietnam’s most atmospheric and delightful town. Once a major port, it boasts the grand architecture and beguiling riverside setting that befits its heritage, and the 21st-century curses of traffic and pollution are almost entirely absent.
